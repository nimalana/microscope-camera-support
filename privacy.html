Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2026

Microscope Camera ("the app") is designed with privacy as a default. This policy explains what data the app does and does not handle.

Data Collection

Microscope Camera does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app has no analytics, no tracking, no advertising, and no third-party SDKs. It does not communicate with any server.

Photos and Videos

Photos and videos you capture in the app are saved only to your device's local Photo Library. The app does not upload, copy, or otherwise transmit your captures anywhere. Files remain on your device under your control.

Permissions

The app requests the following iOS permissions only to provide its core functionality:

  • Camera: to display the live camera preview and capture photos and videos.
  • Microphone: to record audio while capturing video.
  • Photo Library: to save your captures and display the most recent thumbnail.

You can review or revoke these permissions at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
