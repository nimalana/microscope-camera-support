Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

Microscope Camera ("the app") is designed with privacy as a default. This policy explains how the app handles information when you use it, including if you use the app in France, Italy, or another country in the European Union or European Economic Area.

Data Collection

Microscope Camera does not collect personal data. The app operates on your device and does not transmit photos, videos, audio, settings, identifiers, analytics, or other personal information to external servers.

Because the app does not collect personal data, it does not sell personal data, share personal data for cross-context behavioral advertising, or use personal data for profiling. The app has no analytics, no tracking, no advertising, and no third-party SDKs.

Photos and Videos

Photos and videos you capture in the app are saved only to your device's local Photo Library. The app does not upload, copy, or otherwise transmit your captures anywhere. Files remain on your device under your control.

You control any sharing of captured media through iOS features, such as Photos, AirDrop, Messages, Mail, or other apps you choose to use.

Permissions

The app requests the following iOS permissions only to provide its core functionality:

  • Camera: to display the live camera preview and capture photos and videos through a microscope eyepiece adapter. Camera data is not transmitted off your device by the app.
  • Microphone: to record audio while capturing video, such as narrating what you are observing. Audio is stored in the video files you save and is not transmitted off your device by the app.
  • Photo Library: to save your captures, browse microscope photos and videos on your device, and display the most recent thumbnail.

You can review or revoke these permissions at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security.

European Union and EEA Users

For users in France, Italy, and the rest of the European Union or European Economic Area, the app is designed to avoid collecting personal data. Since the app does not collect or process personal data on external servers, requests such as access, correction, deletion, portability, or objection generally apply to data stored on your own device and can be handled by deleting media or the app from your device.

If you contact the developer for support, any information you choose to provide in that communication will be used only to respond to your request.

Educational and Non-Diagnostic Use

The app is intended for educational, hobbyist, documentation, and general microscope imaging use. It is not intended to diagnose, treat, prevent, or monitor any disease or medical condition, and it is not a medical device.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children. In the European Union, where parental consent rules may apply to children under the relevant local age of digital consent, the app is designed not to collect personal data from children or adults.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
